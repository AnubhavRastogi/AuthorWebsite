About me

Anjana was born and raised in the historical town of Agra in India. Agra located on the banks of river Yamuna is best known for the iconic ‘Taj Mahal’. She considers her family, friends, and her school life at St. Patricks Junior College to be the most important to her. She studied in the prestigious St. Patricks Junior College in Agra established in the year 1842. It is the first convent in Asia and now more than 200 years old. Her educational background and avid traveling to places far and wide have given her a broad base from which to approach many topics. She feels poetry can change the world and poems carry an important message, dealing with various issues of that time. She loves her family and friends and loves to spend time with them. She is very fond of her little pug- Nutty who is loved and pampered by her. She is actively involved with welfare of stray animals.

Way Beyond Me

"To all amazing women whom I have met and those I haven't.
Who struggle and battle with dark days. Stay strong woman.
Accept your feelings, grieve your losses,
No one can take away from you, what you have in your mind.
The key is in your own pocket.
You are the fire, the earth, the sweet scented breeze,
the brightest star in the Universe. Never ever give up.
Speak up, show up. Do not let the fire in your eyes dim.
Your story is incredible because you are incredible.
You are the queen. Sing your unsung song, burn the ludgement to dust,
collect yourself piece by piece, walk away from pain,
rom hurt, with your head held high."

-Anjana Bhatia Khanduja.
